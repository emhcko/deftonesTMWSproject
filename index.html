ABOUT DEFTONES

Deftones is an American alternative metal band formed in Sacramento, California, in 1988. Known for their unique blend of heavy metal, alternative rock, and experimental sounds, Deftones have released several critically acclaimed albums, including White Pony and Diamond Eyes. Their music often explores themes of emotional depth, introspection, and the human experience.

MEMBERS

  • Chino Moreno - Vocals, Guitar
  • Stephen Carpenter - Guitar
  • Abe Cunningham - Drums
  • Frank Delgado - Keyboards, Turntables
  • Sergio Vega - Bass

Deftones have been influential in the alternative metal scene and continue to captivate audiences with their innovative sound and powerful live performances.
